New York Style Banana Dog

A delicious treat from the smog infested streets of New York

A stark contrast from the regular hotdogs I would eat on my commute - Some Dude at Penn Station

One of the meals of all time - New York Times

Ingredients

  • Banana (preferably green for the firmness)
  • Hotdog bun, moldy for that extra protein
  • Any choice of toppings, mustard will do
  • An apartment in NYC

Recipe Instructions

  1. Take the banana and peel it
  2. Insert banana into hotdog bun
  3. Drizzle banana dog in any topping of your choice
  4. Let the dish sit on your balcony or fire escape to absorb the smokey smog flavor of NYC
  5. Enjoy your NYC style banana dog
