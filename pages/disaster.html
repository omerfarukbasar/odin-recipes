A Recipe For Disaster

Almost like driving of a cliff

Where did it all go wrong? - Anonymous

Ingredients

  • Anxiety
  • Depression
  • 90 minute commutes, one way
  • Academically rigorous graduate program

Recipe Instructions

  1. Get accepted into Cornell
  2. Get to know everyone and the program
  3. Realize you don't like the computer science courses here
  4. Try to get help with the abundant homework only to get none
  5. Sit and cry by the East River
  6. Eventually drop out
  7. Have hard time getting job interviews
  8. Discover The Odin Project which rekindles your interest in programming
  9. Go through the course up until the recipe project
  10. Stop and recognize the fourth wall break
